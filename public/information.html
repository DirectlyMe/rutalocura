Information

Ordering:

Our shopping cart is operated by PayPal. You DO NOT need a PayPal account to use the shopping cart though. Orders for standard products should be place through our shopping cart. We can not accept credit card numbers over the phone. If you would like to place an order for custom or non standard items, please make the inquiry by email. We will send you an invoice to make payment for such orders, via email. The invoice can be paid by PayPal, or credit card. Feel free to place a phone call also, but an email helps us keep track of things better.

If you would like to place an order via snail mail and a check, use our shopping cart to figure the shipping. Either print out, or write down your order, and mail it to:

  • Ruta Locura LLC
  • 1108 5th st.
  • Ogden, Utah 84404

Shipping:

We ship world wide. We normally ship via United States Postal Service. If you need other shipping arrangements, please contact us before placing your order.
